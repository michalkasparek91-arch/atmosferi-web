Meridian Tower

A launch platform for a 41-storey residential tower — the building sold from the browser, floor by floor.

View the live site ↗
● REC N 50°05′ E 14°25′ F2.8 · 1/250 · ISO 100
The brief

Sell a tower that does not yet exist. We were asked to make 280 unbuilt apartments legible, desirable, and reservable — an instrument for the sales team and a place for buyers to inhabit the view before the concrete cured.

The site pairs an interactive floor-stack with cinematic visualisation. Availability updates from the CRM; the rest stays silent.

Scope
  • Web design
  • Development
  • 3D & visualisation
  • Interactive floor-stack
  • CMS & CRM integration

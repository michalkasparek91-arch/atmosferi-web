The studio Est. MMXXIV · Prague

We design the conditions, not the decoration.

Atmosferi is a small web studio working only with people who build space. We design and develop websites — and render the unbuilt — for architecture, development, urbanism and interiors.

How we work Four principles
01 The architecture leads The interface frames the work and then disappears. Colour belongs to the photograph and the render — never the chrome around it.
02 Drawn to specification We treat a website like a building — measured, gridded, and load-bearing. Typographic tension and flush alignment do the structural work.
03 Design and build, one studio No hand-off between a design shop and a dev shop. The people who draw it are the people who build it — and visualise it.
04 Made to last Permanent, accessible, performant. A site that outlives the launch and is yours to run, like a structure you can maintain.

Live

Homes for everyone

Studios to family townhouses, with a third set aside as affordable and shared-ownership.

Work

The makers' wharf

Affordable studios for 35 small makers, a food hall and a co-working loft in the old grain store.

Gather

A tidal pool, a lido, a weekly market and 1.2 km of boardwalk that stays public, always.

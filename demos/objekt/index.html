Three convictions hold every project together.

A

Material before colour

We choose the stone, the timber and the cloth first; colour is whatever they already are. Nothing is painted to pretend.

B

Light is the furniture

Every plan begins with the path of the sun. A room is arranged around its best hour, then quietly serves the rest.

C

Built to be lived in

An interior is not a photograph. We design for wear, for years, for the marks a life leaves — and the rooms only improve.

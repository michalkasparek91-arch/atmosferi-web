The brief
is a city.

We start every project by zooming out. A tower is a question about a skyline; a school is a question about a neighbourhood; a station is a question about a region.

Our work is pragmatic utopianism — ambitious enough to change how a place works, grounded enough to be built on budget and on time.

Across 38 countries the constant is method: research, test, model, build. We design the systems; the people make them a place.

Architecture practice · Bergen, NO

Quiet buildings,
made for the Nordic light.

A small studio of six. We work in timber and daylight — houses, cabins and the occasional small public room — drawn slowly, detailed by hand.

Est. 2009 · Selected 2019—2026

We believe a building should ask little and give much — warmth, shelter, a frame around the day, and silence enough to hear the weather.

How we work

01 Slowly, and few

We take three or four projects a year so each is drawn by the people who designed it.

02 In timber, mostly

A single honest material, detailed to age well and to be repaired rather than replaced.

03 Around the light

The plan begins with where the sun falls in winter. Everything else follows the daylight.

04 With the land

We touch the ground lightly and let the landscape keep the last word.

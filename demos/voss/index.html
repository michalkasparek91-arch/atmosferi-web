The studio method

We design around the light.

Every plan begins with where the sun falls in winter. Walk a year through one of our rooms and watch the daylight change — in Bergen it is never the same hour twice.

The studio

Six people,
drawing by hand.

We are deliberately small. Every project is led from sketch to site by the same architect, and most of our drawings still begin in pencil before they become a building.

From a top-floor studio on Strandgaten we work across western Norway — and travel to every site, in every season, before we draw a line.

Led by Ingrid Voss & Henrik Sand
